FAQ

What to Expect

How long are surf lessons?

Most lessons are around 2 hours from check-in. With yoga add-on: about 2.5 hours total.

Where do lessons happen?

Common locations include Indian Beach in Ecola State Park, Short Sands, and other local beaches depending on conditions.

What should I bring?

Water, towel, sunscreen, beach layers, and your own transportation. For Indian Beach, plan for park entry fees.

Can I book group lessons?

Yes. Private groups are available, including larger parties and camps. Contact directly for scheduling.

How far in advance should I reserve?

Earlier is best. 24+ hours in advance is recommended for both lessons and rentals.

What if surf conditions are not safe?

Safety is always first. Lessons may be rescheduled or adjusted with refund options depending on conditions.
