Card Change

Hold an ace of clubs in frame. This trick app uses direct image matching and makes the card appear to change into the four of hearts on camera.

On first load, the app compiles ace-of-clubs image targets from bundled reference art. After that, the tracker locks to that face instead of guessing from a loose rectangle.

Best results come from the front camera, a single ace of clubs, and steady lighting with the full card visible.
