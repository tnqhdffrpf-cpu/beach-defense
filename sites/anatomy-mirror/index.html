Card X-Ray

Hold the back of a playing card in frame. This trick app uses MindAR image tracking and fades in a transparent reveal showing a hidden four of hearts through the back.

On first load, the app compiles a Bicycle card-back target from the bundled reference image. After that, the tracker locks to that image target instead of guessing from hand pose or a loose rectangle.

Best results come from the rear camera, a single blue Bicycle-style card back, and steady lighting with the full card visible.
