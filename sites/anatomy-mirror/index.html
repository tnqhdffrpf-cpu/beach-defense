Card X-Ray

Hold the back of a playing card in frame. This trick app tracks the held card and fades in a transparent reveal showing a hidden four of hearts through the back.

The card pose is estimated from hand tracking, then the reveal is mapped onto that card so it feels locked to it as you move.

Best results come from one hand holding a single card in portrait orientation between the thumb and index finger.
