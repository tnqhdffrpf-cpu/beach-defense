iPhone SwiftUI App

Mind Reader Cards

A performance-style card magic trick for iPhone: the spectator thinks of one card from a timed set, the app “shuffles” and removes one, then reveals a new set where their card has vanished.

Project location: SquishBuddy/SquishBuddy/ContentView.swift

Flow:

  • 5-second “think of a card” timer with six cards.
  • Animated shuffle phase with visible card removal.
  • Reveal phase flips five replacement cards one-by-one.
  • Repeat button to run the trick again instantly.
